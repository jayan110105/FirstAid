1. What is a key sign of a fracture?

Mild bruising
Deformity or inability to move limb
Itching

2. What should you do if someone has a suspected sprain?

Apply ice and rest the injured area
Keep walking on it to reduce stiffness
Soak the injured joint in hot water

3. What is the first step of splinting a fracture

Attempt to re align the bonea
Find padding and wrap it around the limb to keep the patient comfortable
Keep the injured area still and find a firm object to use as a split

4.In which direction to we wrap a sprained limb?

Distal End to Proximal End
Proximal End to Distal End
Either, it does not make a difference

5. How long do you apply ice on a sprain?

Until the injured person feels numb
45 minutes every 4 hours
15-20 minutes every 2 to 3 hours
