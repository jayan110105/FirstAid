☀️ Heat Exposure First Aid Quiz

1. What is a common symptom of heat exhaustion?

High body temperature above 104°F (40°C)
Heavy sweating, weakness, and muscle cramps
Dry skin and confusion

2. What should you do if someone shows signs of heat exhaustion?

Move them to a cool, shaded area and give them water
Cool them rapidly with ice-cold water
Leave them alone to rest in the sun

3. What is the first step in treating heatstroke?

Call emergency services immediately
Encourage slow sipping of water
Apply cool, damp cloths to the body

4. What is the best method to find water in a remote location?

Dig in dry riverbeds or look for dew
Drink any water available without filtering
Wait for rain to collect in a container

5. How can you signal for help in a remote location?

Use a mirror or create large SOS symbols
Shout loudly and wave your arms
Build a large fire and wait for rescue
