1. What is a universal sign of choking?

Coughing loudly
Clutching the throat
Holding the stomach

2. What should you do if a choking person is still coughing?

Encourage them to keep coughing
Perform the Heimlich maneuver immediately
Give them water to drink

3. How do you perform the Heimlich maneuver on an infant?

Perform abdominal thrusts
Give back blows and chest thrusts
Tilt the infant backward and pat their head

4. What should you do if a person becomes unconscious while choking?

Start CPR immediately
Continue giving abdominal thrusts
Shake the person to wake them up

5. What is the first step for a self-Heimlich maneuver?

Lean backward and take deep breaths
Press your abdomen against a sturdy object
Try drinking water to clear the airway
